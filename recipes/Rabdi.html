Rabdi, also called Rabri, is a classic North Indian sweet that’s rich, creamy, and perfectly sweet. To make Rabri, milk is cooked until it reduces to a thick pudding-like consistency, then is flavored with cardamoms, saffron, and your favorite nuts and dried fruits. Here you’ll find the best traditional Rabdi recipe with step-by-step instructions and photos to prepare as a delicious dessert!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • khoa
  • Swetner
  • Nuts
  1. Add 1.25 litres of full fat whole milk in a kadai, and slowly heat the milk on a low
  2. When you will see the cream floating on top, use a spatula to gently move the cream layer to the side of the pot.
  3. Stick the cream to the side of the pan.
